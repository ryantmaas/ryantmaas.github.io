MachFlex Data & Vision

As industrial machines become smarter, integration of high-tech components are driving a need for continuous flex data and vision cables. Belden's solution to this emerging need is a suite of RS-232/RS-485/RS-422 and coaxial cables that are engineered to perform reliably up to 1 million flex cycles.

Product Features & Benefits

Flex Data - 1M Flex paired cables


Serial Data Type: RS-232, RS-485, RS-422
Pair Count: 1, 2, 3, or 4
Conductors: Fine strand #24 AWG flexible
Shielding: Beldfoil + 85% Tinned Copper Braid
Insulation: Foam PE with Skin
Jacketing: PVC (Green); -20℃ to 60℃
Approvals: UL and C(UL) CM, UL Oil Res I & II, CE & RoHS, CA Prop 65, 300V RMS

Flex Vision - 1M flex coaxial


RG Type: Sub-mini, mini, RG59, RG6. RG11
Conductors: Fine strand coax; #30, #26, #22, #20, #16 AWG sizes
Shielding: 95% Tinned Copper Braid
Insulation: Foam PE
Jacketing: PVC (Blue); -40℃ to 80℃
Approvals: UL AWM Style 1354, 30V CSA AWM I/II A/B FT1, CE & RoHS, CA Prop 65, 30V RMS
